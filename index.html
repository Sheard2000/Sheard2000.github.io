About Me 

I started photography when I was a young teenager. I went to Bob Jones University and graduated with a bachelors in photography. I then went on to start my business doing infants and engagements and then added other styles such as weddings, family portraits, headshots, and graduation photos. Photography is my passion and what gets me out of bed in the morning!
